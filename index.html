About Us

RetrofitNY is a capstone consulting project commissioned by the Building Energy Exchange of New York City and conducted by candidates of the Master of Public Administration in Environmental Science & Policy (MPA-ESP) '15. MPA-ESP is a graduate program at the School of International & Public Affairs of Columbia University.
